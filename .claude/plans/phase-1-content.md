# Phase 1 Plan: Content & Structure Overhaul

## Goal
Replace all placeholder content with real copy and restructure the page so it leads with the offer, builds trust through social proof, and ends with the artist story.

## New Page Structure

### 1. Hero
- **Headline:** "Make Your Special Day Unforgettable"
- **Visual:** Autoplay video reel — real weddings, real performances, vibrant and joyful (inspired by Laveena Ashish)
- **CTA:** "Plan My Sangeet" or "Start the Conversation"

### 2. What We Offer
Lead with the customer — this is for everyone at the wedding, not just the couple.

- **Framing:** Custom Indian wedding choreography for couples, families, bridal parties, and groom's parties — everyone included
- **What's included end-to-end:**
  - Song selection (client picks any song they want)
  - Custom choreography and formations tailored to the group
  - Teaching and rehearsals until everyone feels confident
- **Tone:** Approachable — no dance experience needed, every body welcome

### 3. Services Breakdown
Individual cards for each offering. Possible categories:
- Couples / First Dance
- Bridal Party
- Groom's Party
- Family Group (sangeet flashmob style)
- Full Wedding Package (combines above)

Each card: short description, what's included, who it's for.

### 4. Video Testimonials
- Autoplay or click-to-play video from real clients
- Quote underneath each video
- Name + wedding date (builds credibility and recency)

### 5. About the Choreographer
- Photo of Romoli (real, professional or high-quality candid)
- Bio highlights:
  - Appeared on America's Got Talent
  - Blends semi-classical, Bollywood, and Bhangra
  - Experience teaching people of all skill levels
- Keep it warm and personal, not a resume
