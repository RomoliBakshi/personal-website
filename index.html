About

Meet the choreographer, Romoli

It all started with friends and family — helping people who'd never danced before find their footing, laugh through rehearsals, and absolutely own the floor on the big day. I know wedding dances are the last thing on your to-do list, but they're often the most memorable moment of the night: a chance to bond with your people, have a little fun, and create something everyone talks about for years.

Romoli has been dancing and choreographing for 25 years, trained in Bharatanatyam, Bollywood, Bhangra, and Bengali folk. She's performed classical, semi-classical, and folk routines in venues and competitions across the US — including a semi-finalist run with her Bhangra team on America's Got Talent Season 9, and 1st place finishes in NJ competitions for both Bharatanatyam and Bhangra. She's currently a professional dancer with Ajna Dance Company in New York, NY.
